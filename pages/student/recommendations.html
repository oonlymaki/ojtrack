About the Office

The Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) San Simon is the primary fire safety and emergency response authority for the Municipality of San Simon, Pampanga. As an OJT intern here, you will work alongside active fire personnel and administrative staff in fire prevention, community outreach, record management, and emergency preparedness. This placement provides hands-on exposure to public safety operations in a government setting.

What You'll Do

Fire Prevention & Inspection

Assist fire personnel in conducting fire safety inspections in establishments and residential areas.

Administrative Support

Encode and file official reports, correspondence, incident records, and permit applications.

Community Outreach

Support fire safety awareness campaigns in barangays, schools, and public events.

Emergency Preparedness

Help organize and document fire drills, evacuation procedures, and first aid demonstrations.

Records Management

Maintain and update fire incident logs, fire safety permits, and personnel records.

Permit Processing Assistance

Guide applicants through fire safety permit requirements and assist in processing applications.
